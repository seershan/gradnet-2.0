About GradNet

Connecting Alumni, Empowering Futures

Our Mission

At GradNet, we believe in the power of connections. Our mission is to create a vibrant, supportive community that empowers alumni to achieve their professional goals and give back to their alma mater.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Seershan Mitra, GradNet was born out of a vision to bridge the gap between academic achievement and professional success. Recognizing the untapped potential of alumni networks, Seershan set out to create a platform that would foster meaningful connections and opportunities for graduates worldwide.

What We Do

  • Connect alumni from diverse backgrounds and industries
  • Provide exclusive job opportunities and career resources
  • Showcase inspiring success stories from our community
  • Facilitate mentorship programs and knowledge sharing
  • Support alma maters through fundraising initiatives

Our Values

  • Community: We believe in the strength of collective support and shared experiences.
  • Innovation: We continuously evolve to meet the changing needs of our alumni.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our interactions.
  • Empowerment: We strive to provide the tools and resources necessary for personal and professional growth.

Join Us

Whether you're a recent graduate or a seasoned professional, GradNet is your platform to connect, grow, and give back. Join our community today and be part of a network that's shaping the future of alumni relations.
